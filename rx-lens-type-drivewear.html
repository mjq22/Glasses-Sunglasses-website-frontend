Transitions Drivewear continuously adapt to changing daylight conditions. Compared to basic sunglasses, Drivewear allows you to work, play, and drive earlier in the morning, later in the evening - in rain, fog, snow or sun and everything in between. In addition, Transitions Drivewear lenses are Polarized, helping to block blinding glare for increased visual acuity, color contrast, and comfort to enhance driving safety and outdoor sporting clarity.

  • (1) Low Light and Overcast Conditions: Drivewear lenses are a green/yellow color offering high contrast while outdoors or driving.
  • (2) For Daytime Driving: This unique technology allows Drivewear lenses to darken to a copper color behind the windshield of the car.
  • (3) In Bright Light and Outside Conditions: Transitions Drivewear lenses change to a dark-reddish brown when outdoors, helping filter excess light and provide increased protection from eye fatigue and impaired vision in bright light."
